# Demo Prompts

Sample prompts to exercise the CortexDJ chat flow end-to-end.

## Session Exploration

> Show me my EEG sessions

Lists all recorded sessions with timestamps, participant IDs, and segment counts.

## Session Analysis

> Analyze session [paste an ID from the list]

Runs the full analysis pipeline: per-segment arousal/valence classification, band power distribution, and emotion quadrant breakdown.

## Brain State Explanation

> What was my brain doing during that session?

Provides a natural-language summary of brain activity patterns, dominant emotional states, and notable transitions.

## Playlist Curation

> Build me a relaxation playlist

Queries historical EEG data for tracks associated with low-arousal, high-valence brain states and assembles a mood-matched playlist.

## Session Comparison

> Compare sessions [ID1] and [ID2]

Side-by-side comparison of two sessions: differences in dominant emotions, band power profiles, and arousal/valence distributions.
